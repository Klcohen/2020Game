Everything is on Fire: The 2020 Wildfire Edition

A Firefighter EMT Training Choose-Your-Own-Adventure Game

Here are the rules

During this choose-your-own-adventure story, you are a firefighter with EMT certification living in San Francisco during the chaos of 2020. It is up to you to decide how you want to respond to scenarios, which are based on firefighter training to improve your decision-making and situational awareness skills. Throughout the game, you will be given scenarios that have 2-3 choices. You must choose one option in under 90 seconds but keep in mind your decision will impact the outcome of the game. Challenge: There are 5 outcomes of the game- see if you can find them all!
