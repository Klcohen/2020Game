Everything is on Fire

Below lists a few methods firefighters use to mitigate the spread of wildfires:

Personnel
Engine strike team
Five fire engines with a lead vehicle
3 or 4 firefighters on each truck
In the game, the firefighter from San Francisco would follow the engine strike team format


Fire Strike team
These firefighters are heavily trained and can respond to fires without additional support or very minimal.
Minimal staffing is 30 firefighters

Control line
Natura Wildfire (e.g on a river or a cliff)
A natural landscape could prevent the spread of the fire by creating an environment in which future spread is hindered

Humanmade Wildfire
Firefighters could purposely burn brush or dead vegetation in order to reduce the fuel that the fire could use to spread
It is important to note that when a fire that is considered contained means the fire has been merely contained within the control line or the perimeter set up of the fire.

Backburn
This approach is typically set up after a control line is established. Firefighters perform a controlled burn inside an area and push the fire up to the main fire while burning all the fuel or dead vegetation in between.
